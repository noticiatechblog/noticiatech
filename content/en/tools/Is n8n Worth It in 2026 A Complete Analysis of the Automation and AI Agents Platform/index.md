*Comparison of modern enterprise automation platforms.*

**Zapier** remains one of the most accessible options for beginners.

Meanwhile, **n8n** stands out for its flexibility and its ability to serve as operational infrastructure.

### When should you choose n8n?

**n8n** is often the best option when organizations require:

- Complex integrations;
- Extensive API usage;
- AI-driven projects;
- Self-hosting capabilities;
- Advanced workflow control.

For smaller automations with minimal technical complexity, simpler platforms may offer faster deployment and easier onboarding.

The right choice depends on technical maturity, budget, and long-term business goals.
