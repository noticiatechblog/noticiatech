## What is Make and why has it become a leading automation platform?

![imagem-1](imagem-1.webp)

*Make uses visual workflow automation to connect applications, streamline business processes, and integrate Artificial Intelligence services.*

**Make** is a visual automation platform that enables businesses to connect multiple applications through graphical workflows known as scenarios. Instead of performing repetitive manual tasks, organizations can design automated processes that transfer information seamlessly across different systems.

This approach has made **Make** a popular solution for companies seeking to eliminate repetitive work, integrate software ecosystems, and accelerate digital transformation without developing custom integrations from scratch.

### Visual automation with enterprise-level flexibility

One of **Make's** biggest advantages is the freedom to design highly customized workflows using an intuitive visual interface.

Beyond simple integrations, users can implement conditional logic, filters, variables, data transformations, and multiple execution paths within the same automation scenario.

### Built for the AI era

Another reason behind Make's rapid adoption is its seamless integration with modern **Artificial Intelligence** services.

Organizations can connect platforms such as **OpenAI**, **Anthropic**, and other AI providers to automate document analysis, generate content, classify information, and power intelligent customer interactions.

## When is Make a better choice than other automation platforms?

![imagem-3](imagem-3.webp)

*Make stands out in projects that require advanced visual workflows, complex integrations, and growing adoption of Artificial Intelligence.*

**Make** is best suited for organizations that need flexible automation, seamless integration between multiple business systems, and detailed control over every step of their workflows. Its visual interface allows teams to build sophisticated automation without requiring extensive software development expertise.

Although many automation platforms compete in this space, **Make** has positioned itself as one of the strongest options by combining usability, scalability, and enterprise-grade flexibility.

### When Make delivers the highest return

The platform is particularly valuable for organizations looking to:

- automate sales operations;
- integrate CRM, ERP, and marketing platforms;
- connect **Artificial Intelligence** services;
- eliminate repetitive operational tasks;
- create custom workflows across multiple systems;
- accelerate digital transformation initiatives.

For businesses that rely on numerous cloud applications, replacing manual work with automated workflows can save dozens of hours every month while reducing operational errors.

### When another platform may be a better fit

Not every organization requires the advanced flexibility that **Make** provides.

Companies with very simple automation needs may find lightweight solutions sufficient during the early stages of adoption. Likewise, highly technical teams that prefer complete infrastructure control may choose open-source platforms that can be self-hosted and extensively customized.

Regardless of the platform selected, the long-term trend is clear: AI-powered automation is becoming a fundamental layer of modern business operations as organizations increasingly connect data, applications, and intelligent decision-making.
