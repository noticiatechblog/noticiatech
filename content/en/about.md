# About Noticia Tech

Noticia Tech is a blog dedicated to exploring how artificial intelligence is transforming the business world.

Our goal is simple: to translate the main AI trends, tools and strategies into practical, direct and applicable content for companies, entrepreneurs and professionals who want to grow with technology.

## Our mission

Turn information into competitive advantage.

## Who this blog is for

- Entrepreneurs
- Marketing and sales professionals
- Managers
- People interested in AI

The world is changing fast, and those who understand it first move ahead.
